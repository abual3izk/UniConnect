Why We Built UniConnect

We developed this system to address three major gaps:

  • Weak graduate tracking:
    Universities struggle to obtain accurate and up-to-date information about the employment status of their graduates.
  • For graduates – communication issues:
    Graduates often lack an official and organized channel to request services or follow up on post-graduation matters.
  • For employers – limited connection with universities:
    Companies face difficulties sharing feedback, verifying certificates, or sending job and internship requests.

General Objective of the Project

The main objective of the project is to build a unified platform that connects graduates, employers, and the university in a single integrated system.

The platform aims to:

  • Centralize data instead of using scattered tools.
  • Improve graduate tracking and employment visibility.
  • Provide an official communication channel for alumni.
  • Help the university enhance its academic programs based on employer feedback.
  • Streamline hiring, verification, and service requests.

How UniConnect Helps Its Users

For Graduates

  • Create a complete professional profile.
  • Apply for jobs approved and monitored by the university.
  • Communicate officially with the university.
  • Track training experiences and employment history.

For Employers

  • Access a reliable pool of qualified graduates.
  • Instantly verify certificates through the system.
  • Easily submit internship and job requests.
  • Provide feedback that helps improve academic programs.

For the University

  • Monitor graduate outcomes and employment trends.
  • Improve communication with alumni.
  • Receive structured feedback from employers.
  • Access accurate reports to support curriculum development.

Why We Chose This Solution

  • It solves real communication gaps between the three main stakeholders.
  • It is scalable and can be extended to more colleges and employers.
  • It establishes a formal, reliable, and centralized communication channel.
